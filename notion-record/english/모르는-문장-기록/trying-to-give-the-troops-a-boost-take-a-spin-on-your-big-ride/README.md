# Trying to give the troops a boost / take a spin on your big ride

# 문장

Trying to give the troops around here a little bit of a boost, and I was thinking that maybe we could take them down to take a spin on your big ride.

# 전체 의미

여기 사람들/팀원들 사기를 조금 북돋아 주려고 하는데, 네 큰 탈것/놀이기구를 한 번 타보게 데려가면 어떨까 생각했어.

# 문장 구조

1. `Trying to give the troops around here a little bit of a boost`
    - `I'm trying to ~`에서 `I'm`이 생략된 구어체 표현
    - `give + 사람 + 사물` 구조
    - `give the troops around here a little bit of a boost` = 여기 사람들에게 약간의 힘/격려를 주다
2. `and I was thinking that maybe we could take them down to take a spin on your big ride`
    - `I was thinking that maybe ~` = 혹시 ~하면 어떨까 생각했어
    - `could`를 써서 제안을 부드럽게 만듦
    - `take them down to ~` = 그들을 데리고 ~로 가다
    - `to take a spin` = 한 번 타보려고 / 한번 체험해보려고

# 성분 나누기

```
Trying to give the troops around here a little bit of a boost,
= I'm trying to give the troops around here a little bit of a boost.
  S  V          to V  IO                      DO
```

```
I was thinking that maybe we could take them down to take a spin on your big ride.
S V             O(that절)     S     V     O    Adv  목적(to부정사구)
```

# 핵심 표현

- `troops` : 원래는 병사들이지만, 여기서는 농담처럼 직원들/팀원들/사람들
- `boost` : 힘, 격려, 사기 상승
- `give someone a boost` : 누군가에게 힘을 주다, 기운 나게 하다
- `take someone down to ~` : 누군가를 ~로 데리고 가다
- `take a spin` : 한 번 타보다, 한 번 몰아보다, 체험해보다
- `big ride` : 큰 놀이기구/탈것. 문맥에 따라 차, 놀이기구, 어트랙션처럼 해석 가능
